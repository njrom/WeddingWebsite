FAQ

What should I wear?

Our wedding dress code is cocktail attire. For women, this typically means a midi- or knee-length dress or a stylish jumpsuit. For men, a suit or blazer with dress pants and a tie is appropriate. We encourage everyone to dress up but also be comfortable enough to celebrate with us!

Will your wedding be indoors or outdoors?

Both our ceremony and reception will take place outdoors, with the reception held under a tent. Since our wedding is in July, you can expect warm and humid weather. We recommend dressing comfortably and considering lighter fabrics to stay cool throughout the day!

What is the best way to get to the venue?

There is plenty of parking available at the venue for those who plan to drive. If you prefer not to drive, ride-share services as well as local taxi options are available in the area.
